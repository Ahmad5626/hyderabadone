ONE HYDERABAD -

One Hyderabad is a luxurious project in Somajiguda, a great investment for your High-living dreams. Keeping the growing demand and need for environment-friendly lifestyles in mind, ‘One Hyderabad’ is meticulously designed where you can experience sustainable luxury, aiming to give back to nature.

One Hyderabad consists of 24 floors perfectly planned to coexist harmoniously with nature, minimizing its environmental footprint while upholding the highest standards of luxury living..

Finest green architecture with luxury -

Let’s change the world by fulfilling a rising need for eco-friendly living

  • The building’s architecture is enhanced with clay mud bricks, naturally cooling the interior while glorifying its aesthetic appeal.

  • Only 29% of the land is occupied by the building, with the remaining space dedicated to lush green areas.

  • A 75-meter vertical garden adds a touch of natural beauty to this eco-friendly masterpiece.
